Sciencey Stuff

Various science things from the internet that I think are cool

(Trust me, as a random 30000 year old eldritch deity I am completely 100% qualified to recommend these things)

xkcd

Pretty rad webcomic about not just science. The guy's books are pretty cool too. Funny website, especially the ones that are more than just an image.

n-gon

2D sidescrolling geometric shooting game with elements all based around various physics things. Granted, I know nothing about most of them, but it's still a cool game. It's made by a high school teacher in California, and the rest of his website is pretty neat as well.

PhET Interactive Simulations

Mostly used in my school science class, but also fun to mess around with. My personal favorite is the Energy Skate Park, where you can build tracks that reach insurmountable speeds.
